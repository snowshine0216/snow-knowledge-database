# 看见孩子 (Seeing the Child)

1. 为什么恨不求上进、白眼狼：因为被贴上了这些标签的时刻，感受到了爱的远离
2. 孩子所做的一切，都是为了让我们爱他
3. 成长中的孩子，容易把感受转为自己的认知
4. 看不见孩子行为背后的困境，而是那些标签

> When we label a child ("ungrateful", "lazy"), the child feels love withdrawing — which is the very moment they most need to be *seen*, not judged. Everything a child does is an attempt to earn our love. Labels block us from seeing the real struggle behind the behavior.
