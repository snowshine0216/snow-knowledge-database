# Chapter 9: What Area Has to Do with Slope

Source:
- https://www.3blue1brown.com/lessons/area-and-slope

Essence:
- Slope (derivative) and area accumulation (integral) look unrelated, but are deeply paired.
- If \(A(x)\) measures accumulated area under \(f\), then \(A\)'s slope at \(x\) equals the current height \(f(x)\).
- This visual identity is the geometric heart of the Fundamental Theorem.

Core equations:
$$
A(x)=\int_a^x f(t)\,dt
$$

$$
A'(x)=f(x)
$$

$$
\frac{d}{dx}\left(\int_a^x f(t)\,dt\right)=f(x)
$$
