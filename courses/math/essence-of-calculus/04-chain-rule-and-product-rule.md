# Chapter 4: Chain Rule and Product Rule

Source:
- https://www.3blue1brown.com/lessons/chain-rule-and-product-rule

Essence:
- Product and chain rules are bookkeeping for how small changes propagate.
- Product rule: total change in \(fg\) is contribution from changing \(f\) plus contribution from changing \(g\).
- Chain rule: derivative of a composition is "outer sensitivity times inner sensitivity."

Core equations:
$$
\frac{d}{dx}(f(x)g(x))=f'(x)g(x)+f(x)g'(x)
$$

$$
\frac{d}{dx}f(g(x)) = f'(g(x))\,g'(x)
$$

$$
d(fg)\approx f\,dg+g\,df
$$
