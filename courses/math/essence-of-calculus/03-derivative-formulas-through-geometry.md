# Chapter 3: Derivative Formulas Through Geometry

Source:
- https://www.3blue1brown.com/lessons/derivatives-power-rule
- https://www.3blue1brown.com/lessons/derivatives-trig-functions

Essence:
- Derivative rules can be discovered geometrically, not memorized as magic.
- Power-rule intuition comes from how areas/volumes scale under tiny changes.
- Trig-derivative intuition comes from unit-circle geometry and tiny-angle behavior.

Core equations:
$$
\frac{d}{dx}x^n = nx^{n-1}
$$

$$
\frac{d}{dx}\sin x = \cos x,\qquad \frac{d}{dx}\cos x = -\sin x
$$

$$
\lim_{h\to 0}\frac{\sin h}{h}=1
$$
