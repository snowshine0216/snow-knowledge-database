## Summary
- Breaks down GPT as Generative Pre-Trained Transformer and focuses on the transformer architecture.
- Walks through tokenization, embedding vectors, stacked attention/MLP blocks, and output unembedding.
- Builds geometric intuition for embeddings: directions and dot products correspond to semantic relationships.
- Explains softmax as the final conversion from raw scores to a probability distribution over next tokens.
- Connects architecture to behavior: autoregressive generation comes from repeatedly feeding back sampled outputs.
