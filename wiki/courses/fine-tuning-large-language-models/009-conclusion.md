# Lesson 009 — Conclusion

**Course:** [[_index|Finetuning Large Language Models]] · DeepLearning.AI  
**Instructor:** Sharon Zhou (Lamini)

---

## Summary

Course wrap-up. Having covered the full fine-tuning pipeline — motivation, data preparation, training, and evaluation — practitioners are equipped to apply fine-tuning as a practical tool alongside prompting and RAG.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Fine-tuning pipeline: **Data Preparation → Training → Evaluation** (iterate)
- [[Instruction fine-tuning]] is the mechanism that transformed GPT-3 into ChatGPT — it teaches *response behavior*, not new knowledge
- Start with a **400M–1B parameter** model for a cheap baseline before scaling
- [[LoRA]] makes fine-tuning accessible even when GPU memory is constrained
- Task-specific fine-tuned models will score lower on general benchmarks — this is expected and correct
